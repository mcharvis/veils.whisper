### **White Gown Brooch** _(Wondrous Item, uncommon)_

> _“A token once feared, now simply watched.”_

- While wearing the brooch, you gain **+1 to Insight checks**
    
- Once per day, you can **cast _Guidance_** as a bonus action (no concentration, no verbal component)
    
- At night, you may dream of a **woman in a white gown** standing silently at the edge of a field.  
    She never moves closer. But she always watches.
