_Weapon (Rapier), Rare — Rapier of the Draegar Territory_

A slender rapier forged from dark, almost oily metal that seems to absorb light. Its blade faintly ripples with living shadows, and its crossguard resembles outspread bat wings. When drawn, a soft whisper of distant winds and hushed voices escapes from the blade.

## Optional Effects

**Magic Weapon.** +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon.

**Necrotic Edge.** On a hit, deals an extra 1d4 necrotic damage.

**Shadow Grasp.** Once per turn when you hit a creature with this weapon, you may use a bonus action to erupt shadowy tendrils from the wound. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier) or be restrained by shadowy bindings until the end of your next turn. A restrained creature can use its action to repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.

**The Silent Step.** Once per long rest, as a bonus action, you can dissolve into a cloud of monochrome ash. You become Incorporeal until the end of your turn. While in this state:
- You have a flying speed equal to your walking speed.
- You can move through creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain.
- If you end your turn inside an object, you take 1d10 force damage and are shunted to the nearest unoccupied space.

---

## Lore

Legends say the Shadowreign Rapier was crafted in the obsidian forges of Elarith by the Dreadsmith Vel'karyn. It was tempered in the essence of a fallen star and bound to the will of Draegar Lords. Whosoever wields it can bend the darkness as though it were flesh, though prolonged attunement risks one's spirit slipping into the eternal night.
