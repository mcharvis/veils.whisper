_Wondrous Item (Necklace), Rare, Requires Attunement by a Paladin or Warlock_

---

## Description

A dark iron cross on a silver chain. The iron is old — older than it should be, older than Kovinar's memory of receiving it. It does not tarnish. It does not warm to the touch. When held in the hand during prayer, it pulls faintly downward, as if weighted by something that has no mass.

---

## Lore

Forged by [[Caelrith\|Caelrith]], a devoted loyalist of Xaphan, the Cursed Cross was conceived as a soul-harvesting relic — a mechanism for claiming the dead on behalf of Nerull. Xaphan could have wielded it. He refused. His chaotic good nature would not bind itself to Nerull's accounting, even in exchange for divine favor. The cross sat dormant.

Before his surrender to Nerull, Xaphan entrusted it to [[05_Player Characters/Kovinar\|Kovinar]] — a paladin of Bahamut, a man whose grace he believed in. In Elarith, Kovinar attuned it to Bahamut, speaking the words Xaphan had written for him. The relic accepted the attunement. It has served him faithfully since.

---

## Attunement (Kovinar Only)

Attuned by speaking aloud: *"By the grace of Bahamut, may your soul find its true path."*

---

## Effect

**Divine Salvation.** While attuned, Kovinar may cast [[Spells/Divine Salvation\|Divine Salvation]] using the cross as the material component. See the [[Spells/Divine Salvation\|Divine Salvation]] spell entry for full mechanics.

**Wisdom.** The cross grants +3 to Wisdom while in Elarith.
