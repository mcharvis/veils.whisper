**While this item is in your possession:**

When you wrap yourself or one willing creature in the blanket, that creature gains 5 temporary hit points.

---

## History

The red cloth was a piece of [[04_Organizations/The Sanctifiers\|Sanctifier]] ceremonial fabric — torn from a tablecloth still reeking of ash and incense — that [[06_NPCs/Sanctifier Samantha Virelle\|Sanctifier Samantha Virelle]] used to wrap [[06_NPCs/Nasha\|Nasha]] in the moments after her birth in the sub-level storage of [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/Ya'a Thalor\|Ya'a Thalor]]. She held her daughter for exactly one minute before walking to [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/The Whispering Asylum\|The Whispering Asylum]] and leaving the bundle on the threshold.

The blanket was still with [[06_NPCs/Nasha\|Nasha]] when the party found her in the storage room. [[06_NPCs/Sanctifier Samantha Virelle\|Samantha]] took it from the room when she found her daughter's body. She carried it to the Chapel of Vex Ceniza during the resurrection ritual. She held it over [[06_NPCs/Nasha\|Nasha]]'s grave in [[Vis'Avae\|Vis'Avae]] when the ritual failed.

It is described in those who have held it since as still carrying warmth that has no obvious source.
