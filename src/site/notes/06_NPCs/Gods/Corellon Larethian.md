_Chief god of the Seldarine, the elven pantheon. God of magic, art, and elven nobility. Eternal enemy of Lolth._

Corellon is distant the way high gods tend to be, more invoked than encountered, his name carried in oaths and namedays rather than temples full of supplicants waiting on a sign. He is patron of the high elves above the other elven peoples, credited with their gift for magic and their long memory for beauty, and he has stood against Lolth since before either of them had worshippers to keep score.

He has no established interest in dragons, bloodlines, or what runs beneath an elf's skin. That has never come up before.
