_God of Justice, Honor, and Nobility. Eternal enemy of [[Tiamat\|Tiamat]]._

Bahamut is the Platinum Dragon, patron god of metallic dragons and champion of justice. He stands as the divine mirror to [[Tiamat\|Tiamat]]'s tyranny — where she hoards and destroys, he liberates and protects.

[[05_Player Characters/Kovinar\|Kovinar]] swore his Paladin oath to Bahamut after being freed from the corrupting influence of the Green Dragon Mask at Bahamut's temple. The oath did not erase his infernal bond to [[06_NPCs/Malphas\|Malphas]] — but it gave him the strength to wield it rather than be ruled by it.
