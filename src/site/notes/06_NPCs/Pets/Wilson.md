## Overview

Wilson was alive once, a runt pulled from a litter that [[05_Player Characters/Squeaks\|Lurtza]] raised by hand back when she still ran with her mother's warband. He was the only thing in that camp that never treated her as less, and when she finally slipped away under cover of night, taking nothing but her daggers, she took him too.

For years they were inseparable. He darted along her shoulders and squeaked his warnings before danger ever showed itself, the closest thing she had to a second set of eyes in the dark. Then came a winter raid, a trap set for something far larger than a rat, and Wilson didn't come back from it. Lurtza buried him herself, hands shaking, and swore the world wouldn't take the one bond she trusted from her again.

It already had. What came next was her answer to that.

---

## The Bargain

Drunk, grieving, and not particularly careful about who she was talking to, Lurtza found Xaphan, or he found her. He took an interest in her grief, and a deal was struck: he would bring Wilson back, and in return she'd help him acquire relics he wanted retrieved.

Nights later, the dirt over the grave moved. Wilson clawed his way out of the shallow earth, ribs visible beneath tattered fur, eyes glowing faintly in a way no living rat's ever have. He was still dead. He just hadn't stopped moving.

Lurtza never called it a curse. To her, it was proof, that some loyalty doesn't end just because the body giving it does.

---

## Wilson Today

He still rides on Squeaks' shoulder more often than not, still squeaks first and asks questions never, and still has an unsettling habit of grooming wounds that should have killed him. He doesn't eat, doesn't sleep in any way that resembles rest, and the party has long since stopped being surprised by any of that.
