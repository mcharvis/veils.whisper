**Role:** Keeper of the [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/Chapel of Vex Ceniza\|Chapel of Vex Ceniza]]
**Location:** [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/Oasis\|Oasis]] — the chapel and its churchyard
**Personality:** Morbid, gentle, cryptic. Answers questions that demonstrate you already understand most of the answer.

> *"The dead whisper when the soil is wrong. I've heard them scream."*

---

## What He Is

Oth has tended the [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/Chapel of Vex Ceniza\|Chapel]] since before anyone in [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/Oasis\|Oasis]] can date clearly. He buried his entire family after a plague swept through the city and never left the graveyard after that. Whether this is devotion, guilt, or the particular stubbornness of a man who decided that leaving would be a betrayal of something — he has never said, and probably would not say if asked.

He knows the real history of [[02 World/The Evergrowing Forest/Oasis\|Oasis]]'s founding. He knows what the grey dust on the altar is. He knows it is not ash.

He will not sweep the altar. He says:

> *"That's not ash. That's Ceniza's shadow. Sweep it, and he follows you home."*

He is not speaking metaphorically.

---

## What He Saw

On the night [[06_NPCs/Sanctifier Samantha Virelle\|Samantha Virelle]] came through the oak doors, Oth was outside reinforcing the cemetery wards. He heard her arrive — boots on the road, the doors, the silence that followed. He came in through the vestry door and stood in the dark and watched.

He saw the ritual. He saw the detonation. He saw the figure in the rafters.

He cannot prove it was [[06_NPCs/Malphas\|Malphas]]. He knows what it was.

He did not intervene. He tells himself this is because any intervention would have made things worse. He has not fully decided whether he believes this.

He has told no one. He will tell someone who asks the right questions.

---

## Social Mechanic

He responds to:
- Honesty — not diplomacy, not performance, actual honesty
- Demonstrated knowledge of what the chapel is and what happened here
- Genuine respect for the dead as people rather than as problems or resources

He does not respond to authority, coin, urgency, or flattery.

**DC 15 Insight** before speaking reads the room correctly: he already knows more than the party does. He is deciding whether they deserve to hear it.

**DC 13 History or Religion** on the chapel itself opens the first layer: the Vex title, what Ceniza was, and why the altar dust behaves the way it does.

**Honest engagement + DC 14 Persuasion** (or a direct, unvarnished statement of what the party is trying to do and why) unlocks what he saw the night of the transformation.
