## The Moonflower

In the golden years of [[06_NPCs/King Aldenor\|Aldenor]]'s reign, Aurienne walked beside him as a queen of strange and radiant beauty, violet eyes rimmed in gold, as though twilight itself smoldered in her gaze. The people adored her quiet grace, though among the elder courtiers there were always whispers that her blood was not wholly elven, that no true elf bore eyes that lit like that in the dark. The whispers eventually died out. Her presence and her beauty were taken as divinity, and divinity does not require an explanation.

Her gift was sight beyond sight. She could walk the threads of fate and glimpse what was coming, though the visions came to her sideways, in flashes she could not always order or trust. In the final months of her reign, one vision would not leave her: a curse that would one day fall on her daughter, wrapped in golden flame. She did not know how she knew it. She only knew it was true.

She told [[06_NPCs/King Aldenor\|Aldenor]] in a low voice, late at night: *"I saw Vespertina die. And the flame that saved her came from me."*

Believing her own blood the cause of that doom, Aurienne withdrew from court. Her magic dimmed. Her presence softened, as if she were fading from the world by choice rather than waiting for it to take her.

---

## The Disappearance

One night, she climbed to the high terrace, clothed in white, her hair caught in the wind. Witnesses said she spoke softly to the air, as if answering someone unseen: *"If mercy breeds suffering, let me bear it first."*

A golden light rose from her skin, gentle, not blinding, until her form dissolved into motes of lavender and gold that drifted into the night sky. No body was found. Only a faint spiral mark, burned into the marble beneath her feet.

[[06_NPCs/King Aldenor\|Aldenor]] never spoke of her as a widower speaks of grief. He wore no shroud, and the realm mourned an absence with no tomb to grieve over.

Bards have sung of her fate since. Some say she wanders still, a ghost caught between life and death, searching for the key to unravel her own daughter's destiny. Others say she waits for [[05_Player Characters/Vespertina\|Vespertina]] to come find her.
