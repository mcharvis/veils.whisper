## **Lucian, the Oath Rekindled**

_Medium humanoid (any), lawful good_  
**CR 4 (1,100 XP)**

| AC  | HP  | Speed |
| --- | --- | ----- |
| 18  | 68  | 30 ft |

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|17 (+3)|12 (+1)|18 (+4)|10 (+0)|13 (+1)|15 (+2)|

**Saving Throws** Str +6, Con +7, Wis +4  
**Skills** Athletics +6, Intimidation +5, Persuasion +5  
**Senses** passive Perception 11  
**Languages** Common, Celestial  
**Proficiency Bonus** +3

---

### **TRAITS**

**Aura of Resolve.**  
Lucian and friendly creatures within 10 ft. gain +1 to saving throws and can't be frightened while he's conscious.

**Oath Rekindled (1/Day).**  
When reduced to 0 HP, Lucian makes a DC 15 Charisma saving throw. On a success, he rises with 1 HP at the start of his next turn—his vow burning through the darkness.

**Radiant Conviction (Recharge 5–6).**  
As a bonus action, Lucian channels his will for 1 minute. His melee attacks deal an extra 4 (1d8) radiant damage and shed bright light in 10 ft.

---

### **ACTIONS**

**Multiattack.**  
Lucian makes three melee attacks.

**Longsword.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 9 (1d8 + 5) slashing damage, plus 4 (1d8) radiant damage while Radiant Conviction is active.

**Commanding Shout (Recharge 6).**  
Lucian lets out a defiant cry. Each ally who can hear him gains 5 temporary hit points and advantage on its next attack roll before the start of Lucian's next turn.

---

### **REACTIONS**

**Defend the Vow.**  
When a creature within 5 ft. of Lucian is hit, he can impose disadvantage on the attack roll as he raises his shield.
