# Prophecy XVII
*Shard 14 · ms. BT-027 · second hand*

---

> The ninth is not counted in the counting.
> The nine know of eight.
> The eighth does not know of the ninth.
> The ninth does not want to be found.
> The ninth is the one doing the finding.

---

## Notes on Source and Attribution
*Aldric Senne · Year of the Greying 43*

The second hand on Shard 14 is calm. Unusually so, compared to the other secondary scribes in this collection. The letterforms are even, the pressure consistent. Whatever this person was writing, they were not afraid of it. They may have been the one who knew what it meant.

Nine figures, one hidden from the counting. An organization — an order, a council, a bound company — that believes itself to be eight while one member operates outside their knowledge. The eighth does not know of the ninth. The group's apparent limit is not its actual limit. Someone is watching from a position they have deliberately obscured.

*The ninth does not want to be found.* This is character, not circumstance. The concealment is chosen. And then the final reversal: the ninth is the one doing the finding. The hidden member is not passive. They are the one with agency. The eight who believe they are nine are not the hunters. They are the hunted — and they do not know it.

I have no historical referent for this. Orders of nine are not common in the literature I know. I note it and leave it for scholars better positioned to pursue it.

*— A. Senne*
