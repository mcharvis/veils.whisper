# Prophecy II
*Shard 1 · ms. BT-004 · second hand*

---

> She will not come when called.
> She will come when the calling stops.
> What she brings with her was yours before you were born.

---

## Notes on Source and Attribution
*Aldric Senne · Year of the Greying 41*

A second hand picks up midway through the same shard — the ink changes, the letterforms narrow, the pressure on the stylus increases noticeably. Whatever reason caused the first scribe to stop, the second did not wait long. The text continues without gap or preamble.

The subject here is female. That is rare in the Testimonies. The death-figures of the Fallen Age are overwhelmingly genderless or implicitly male — functionaries of passage, administrators of the end. A female figure this explicitly drawn carries weight. I have considered whether this refers to a specific goddess or divine figure, but the phrasing resists that reading. She is not named. She is not characterized by domain. She is characterized only by her timing and by what she carries.

*What she brings with her was yours before you were born.* I have read this line forty times. I have not resolved it. An inheritance that predates the inheritor is either cosmological — a soul's original nature — or it is a debt. Neither reading makes her benevolent.

*— A. Senne*
