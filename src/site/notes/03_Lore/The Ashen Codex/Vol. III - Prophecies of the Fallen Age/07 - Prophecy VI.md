# Prophecy VI
*Shard 5 · Destroyed*

---

*Shard 5 was among the materials housed in the Vis'Avae archive at the time of the fire in the Year of the Greying 39. It did not survive. The entry is recorded here by catalogue designation only.*

*Two expedition members who had briefly handled the shard before its destruction reported it as the longest single fragment in the collection. Neither could recall specific text. One said only that it concerned water. The other disagreed.*

*No reconstruction has been attempted.*

*— A. Senne, Year of the Greying 41*
