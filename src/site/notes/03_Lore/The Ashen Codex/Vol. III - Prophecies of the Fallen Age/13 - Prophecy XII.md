# Prophecy XII
*Shard 10 · Unrecovered*

---

*Shard 10 has not been located. Its existence is confirmed by the partial catalogue recovered from Vis'Avae, which lists it between Shard 9 and Shard 11 without further description.*

*Its absence is noted. No reconstruction has been attempted.*

*— A. Senne, Year of the Greying 42*
