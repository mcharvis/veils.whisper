# Prophecy III
*Shard 2 · Unrecovered*

---

*Shard 2 has not been located. All three expeditions attempted to trace its provenance from the partial catalogue recovered at Vis'Avae. No physical fragment has been found. Whether it was destroyed, concealed, or simply lost to time is unknown.*

*Its absence is noted. No reconstruction has been attempted.*

*— A. Senne, Year of the Greying 41*
