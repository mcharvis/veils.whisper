# Prophecy VII
*Shard 6 · ms. BT-012*

---

> Two hearts in a jar.
> One beating. One still.
> The still one is the louder.

---

## Notes on Source and Attribution
*Aldric Senne · Year of the Greying 42*

The shortest entry in the collection. Three lines. No preamble, no closing image. It simply ends.

The jar-as-vessel image appears in death-cult ritual practice of the Fallen Age — specifically in the preservation rites documented in Vol. I's liturgical fragments, where organs removed during preparation of the dead were stored in sealed containers for the journey. Two hearts in a single jar is not consistent with standard mortuary practice. It is either error, deliberate deviation from ritual, or something else entirely.

A still heart that is louder than a beating one. I have no satisfactory interpretation. The image refuses reduction. I include it and move on, with the note that this is the entry I am most uncertain I understand.

*— A. Senne*
