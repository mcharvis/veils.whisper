# Prophecy X
*Shard 9 · ms. BT-017*

---

> The child is not lost.
> The child is in the small warm thing.
> The small warm thing does not know.
> The mother does not know.
> The child does not know.
> Three kinds of not-knowing, and still the thread holds.

---

## Notes on Source and Attribution
*Aldric Senne · Year of the Greying 42*

I have spent more time with this entry than any other in the collection. I return to it and I find it changed and I know it is not changed, and I cannot account for the sensation.

The structure is a list of ignorances. Three parties, none of whom understand the situation they are in. The child is contained in something — the small warm thing — and what that container is, the text declines to say. The container does not know it is a container. The mother does not know her child is there. The child does not know where it is. All three in the dark. And yet — and still — the thread holds.

The thread is the only element in this entry that is not ignorant of itself. It simply holds. It does not know or not-know; it functions. Whatever binds these three together operates independently of any of them understanding that they are bound.

I cannot explain why this entry reads to me as consolation. By all rights it should read as horror. A child trapped, a mother who cannot find it, a vessel that doesn't know what it carries. But the last line refuses to be tragic. The thread holds. Whatever that means, it means something is not broken.

I am aware I may be reading comfort into a text that is not offering it. I note it regardless.

*— A. Senne*
