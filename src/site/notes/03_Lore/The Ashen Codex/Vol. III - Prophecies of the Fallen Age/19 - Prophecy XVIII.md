# Prophecy XVIII
*Shard 15 · ms. BT-029*

---

> Beyond the Veil there is a desk.
> At the desk there is a woman who is not alive.
> She is working.
> She has been working for a long time.
> She is almost finished.

---

## Notes on Source and Attribution
*Aldric Senne · Year of the Greying 43*

I have read this entry to my colleagues without comment and watched their faces. It produces a consistent reaction: a brief stillness, then a studied neutrality that is itself a kind of response. No one has offered me an interpretation. I have not pressed.

The Veil again. Beyond it, something domestic — a desk, which is an object of ordinary labor. What waits beyond death is not a throne room or an abyss. It is a workspace. And the worker is not dead — not dead in the absolute sense — but not alive. She occupies a threshold state. And she is working, and she has been working for a long time, and she is almost finished.

Almost finished with what the entry declines to say. Almost finished implies a task with an end. Something she is building, or compiling, or preparing. Something that will be complete.

I do not find this entry consoling. I include that observation not to color the reader's response but because I believe my own reactions to this material constitute data. Whatever my instincts are registering in this entry, they are registering it as warning.

*— A. Senne*
